# News And Disclosure Event Taxonomy

News events:

- Positive-leaning: earnings surprise, guidance raise, major contract, buyback,
  dividend increase, M&A, approval, analyst upgrade, policy benefit.
- Negative-leaning: earnings miss, guidance cut, offering, convertible bond,
  accounting issue, lawsuit, recall, clinical failure, downgrade, delisting risk.
- Neutral or mixed: repeated news, rumor, analyst reiteration, macro commentary,
  unclear event.

Event classification must evaluate novelty, relevance, duplicate risk,
source quality, priced-in risk, price confirmation, and volume confirmation.

LLM output is structured evidence only. It cannot directly set final ranking.
