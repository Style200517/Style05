# Risk Policy

Risk penalty is capped at 35 in MVP 1.

Rules:

- 5-day return >= 20%: +10.
- 20-day volatility over 4.5%: +10.
- 20-day volatility over 3.0%: +5.
- low liquidity: +15.
- 20-day low breakdown: +10.
- data quality issue: +5 to +15.
- weak market regime: +5.

Risk flags must be displayed with the score and label.
